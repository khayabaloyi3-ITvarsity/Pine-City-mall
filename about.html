About Us

Pine City Mall is a retail landmark in the area of Alexandra, Johannesburg. Opened in March 2017, the Mall boasts over 90 stores to cater for all tastes. From local favourites to national anchors it is a guaranteed shopping haven. Over 1 000 parking bays and an additional 500 taxi bays ensure expedient access and egress. We pride ourselves in providing our valued customers with a convenient shopping experience in a safe, secure, clean and friendly environment.
